About Care Medicity

Medical specialty concerned with the care of acutely ill hospitalized patients

We are committed to delivering top-tier medical attention for all acutely ill patients requiring hospitalization. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that every aspect of your recovery is managed with the utmost expertise and advanced medical resources.

Primary Care

Our dedicated primary care physicians provide comprehensive initial health assessments, preventive care, and ongoing management for your family's everyday medical needs.

Lab Test

Our state-of-the-art laboratory provides precise and timely diagnostic testing, essential for accurate disease detection and effective treatment planning.

Symptom Check

Our experienced doctors offer thorough symptom evaluation to help identify underlying conditions and guide you towards the most appropriate medical care.

Heart Rate

We provide accurate heart rate monitoring and comprehensive cardiovascular assessments to help maintain your heart health and detect any potential issues early.
